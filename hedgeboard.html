HOW IT WORKS

Step 1: Get ACA Tokens

Traders and Users purchase tokens with bitcoin on the platform. Alternatively, Users can acquire ACA tokens from an exchange of their choice.

Step 2: Stake ACA Tokens and Create Blueprint

Traders stake ACA tokens when they create their trading prediction or "Blueprint" on the HedgeBoard platform.

Step 3: Users Unlock Blueprint and Await Expiry

Users can unlock the prediction details by purchasing the Blueprint with ACA; they will then wait for the Oracle to resolve the Blueprint at the time of expiry.

Step 4: Blueprint Outcome

The ACA tokens are automatically distributed to the Trader if the Blueprint is correct. If the Blueprint is incorrect, the tokens are returned to the User.
