Which Network is the ACA Token on?

How to buy ACA tokens with Metamask?

A:

  1. Create a metamask wallet
  2. Add the BSC network
  3. Transfer BNB into metamask
  4. Go to https://pancakeswap.finance/swap
  5. Connect your MM wallet
  6. The "from" is BNB and "to" is the ACA token contract (https://www.dextools.io/app/bsc/pair-explorer/0x1e06b8480733505e9dc228f69588511586edaad1) can be found here, just copy and paste
  7. Choose the amount of BNB you want to use to buy ACA
Can’t buy more ACA due to Output Error

I added to the liquidity pool but as there are tons of buying orders for ACA, my ACA amount is getting lower, however my BNB is going up. What happens if the ACA amount goes to 0? Do I lose my liquidity pool position? Or do I just keep collecting from the fees?

You will keep collecting. Your pool will always balance, for example, when there are lots of sell orders, the ACA pool will be increasing again.
