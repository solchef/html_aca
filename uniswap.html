HOW TO BUY ACA TOKEN ON UNISWAP

We are currently using UniSwap V2! please make sure you always switch to V2 while transacting with ACA!

STEP 1 - Install MetaMask Wallet

  • Download MetaMask and install on your browser.
  • You’ll see the MetaMask fox icon next to your address bar.
  • Click the icon and MetaMask will open up.
  • Create a New password. Then, write down the Secret Backup Phrase in a safe place. MetaMask will then create a new Ethereum address for you.

STEP 2 - Copy your Metamask wallet address

  • Everytime you copy and paste an Ethereum address, remember and check that the first 2 digits and last 2 digits are correct!

STEP 3 - Purchase some Ethereum

  • Buy some Ethereum and send the $ETH to your MetaMask wallet address.
  • In your exchange, find the Withdraw option. Select Ethereum. They ask where do you want to send, paste there the Metamask address.

STEP 4 - Purchase $ACA on Uniswap V2

  • Go to Uniswap. Select ETH and click Select a token

  • Select the amount of $ETH you want to spend and you will see the amount of $ACA you will receive
  • Click Swap
  • Metamask will open a new window and ask you for confirmation. Confirm. Then wait until the transaction is completed! Be patient, sometimes it takes a few minutes
  • If everything is correct, you will see the new coins you bought in the bottom Balance
